## Sectioned Settings

The settings page has been redesigned with a new tabbed navigation. Instead of scrolling through a long list of cards, your settings are now organized into clear sections — Profile, Security, Notifications, Calendar, Language, and Data & Privacy. On desktop, a sticky sidebar lets you jump between sections instantly. On mobile, swipeable chips at the top let you navigate with a simple swipe or tap.

![Settings page with sectioned sidebar navigation](./images/settings-navigation.png)

## Improved Mobile Experience

Sectioned pages — including settings and the changelog — now use a swipeable chip navigation on mobile devices. The active section smoothly animates into view, and the page height adapts to the content so you never see unnecessary whitespace.

## Auto-Save for Notification Preferences

Your notification preferences are now saved automatically as you change them — no more clicking a save button. A small indicator at the bottom of the screen confirms when your changes have been saved.

## Deep-Linkable Settings Sections

Each settings section now has its own URL. You can bookmark or share a direct link to a specific section, and the browser back and forward buttons navigate between sections as expected.

## Language Applies to Notifications

The language settings card now shows a hint that your language choice also applies to email notifications and other messages you receive.
