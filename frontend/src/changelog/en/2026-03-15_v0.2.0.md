## Global Notification Toggles

Previously you could only configure notifications one by one. Now there are global toggles that let you turn entire notification channels on or off with a single switch.

Want to silence all email notifications while keeping in-app alerts? Just flip the email toggle off. You can always re-enable it later without losing your individual notification preferences.
