## Push Notifications

You can now receive push notifications directly in your browser — no need to keep the app open to stay in the loop. Head to your notification preferences and enable push notifications to get instant alerts for booking confirmations, schedule changes, and upcoming duties.

## Telegram Integration

Prefer getting updates on your phone? You can now connect your Telegram account and receive duty reminders and notifications as Telegram messages. Link your account from the notification settings page using the Telegram login widget, and you'll start receiving updates right away.

![Notification settings with Push and Telegram channels](./images/notification-settings.png)

## Per-Notification Controls

Each notification type can now be individually configured across all three channels — Email, Push, and Telegram. Choose exactly how you want to be notified for booking confirmations, event publications, admin alerts, and more.

![Full notification preferences with per-type channel toggles](./images/notification-settings-full.png)

## Smaller Improvements

- The notification preferences page now features animated icons that make it easier to see which channels are active at a glance.
- Improved reliability of the notification stream — notifications are now delivered more consistently, even during longer sessions.
