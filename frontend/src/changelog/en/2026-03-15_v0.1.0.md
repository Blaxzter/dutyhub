Welcome to the very first release! This is the foundation of the app — everything you need to organize duty shifts and keep your team in sync.

## Event & Duty Management

Create events, define duty slots with specific time windows, and let your team members book themselves into shifts. Each event shows how many slots are still open, so you can see at a glance where help is needed.

An infinite-scroll quick view lets you browse upcoming events without leaving the page you're on.

![Events overview with inline shift preview](./images/events.png)

## Booking Your Shifts

Open any event to see all available duty slots grouped by day. Slots you've already booked are highlighted with a checkmark, so you always know where you're signed up. Book or cancel with a single click.

![Event detail showing duty slots and your bookings](./images/event-detail.png)

## Dashboard

Your personal dashboard gives you a bird's-eye view of everything that matters: how many events are available, your confirmed bookings, and a full calendar showing when duties are scheduled.

![Dashboard with calendar and quick stats](./images/dashboard.png)

## User Accounts & Approval

New users sign up and wait for admin approval before they can access the app. Admins can activate, deactivate, or remove accounts at any time. If you ever want to leave, you can delete your own account and all associated data from the settings page.

![Admin user management view](./images/user-management.png)

## Notification Preferences

Choose how you want to be notified about booking confirmations, schedule changes, and upcoming duties. You can fine-tune your preferences from the settings page.

## Multi-Language Support

The entire app is available in English and German. Switch languages at any time from your settings — everything updates instantly, including menus, labels, and messages.

## Admin Tools

Admins get a dedicated area for managing users and overseeing all events and bookings. There's also a demo data tool for quickly populating the app with sample events and bookings during setup or testing.
