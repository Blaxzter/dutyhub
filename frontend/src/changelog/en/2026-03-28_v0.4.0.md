## Booking Reminders

You can now set reminders for your bookings so you never miss a shift. Each reminder lets you choose when to be notified and through which channel — for example, a push notification 15 minutes before and an email 1 day before.

New users get sensible defaults out of the box: a push reminder 15 minutes before and an email reminder 1 day before each booking.

![Default reminder settings with per-offset channel selection](./images/default-reminders.png)

## Booking Detail View

Clicking a booking from "My Bookings" now opens a dedicated detail page instead of the slot dialog. The new view organizes everything into clear sections: slot info, your notes, reminders, and co-bookers. Past events show a prominent banner so you know at a glance that it already took place.

![Booking detail view with reminders and notes](./images/booking-detail.png)

## Per-Booking Customization

Default reminders are just a starting point. On the booking detail page, you can add, remove, or change the delivery channel for individual reminders. This way you can get an extra early heads-up for important shifts or skip reminders for ones you won't forget.
