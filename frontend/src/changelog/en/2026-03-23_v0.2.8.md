## Date Range Filter

Your bookings and event group views now have a date range picker. Select a start and end date to narrow down exactly the time period you're interested in — no more scrolling through weeks of past events to find what you need.

![Bookings view with date filter and grouping options](./images/bookings.png)

## Sidebar Quick Links

The sidebar has been updated with quick-access links that take you directly to the most commonly used sections of the app. This saves you a few clicks on every visit, especially on pages buried deeper in the navigation.
