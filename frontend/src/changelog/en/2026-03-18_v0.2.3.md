## Legal Pages

The app now includes dedicated pages for the imprint and privacy policy, accessible from the footer on every page. These provide the legally required information about the service and how your data is handled.

![Privacy policy page](./images/privacy.png)

## Cookie Notice

On your first visit you'll see a cookie notice explaining what cookies the app uses and why. You can review your cookie preferences at any time.

## Personal Data Export

You can now download a copy of all your personal data from your account settings. This gives you full transparency into what information the app stores about you, and helps you stay in control of your data.
