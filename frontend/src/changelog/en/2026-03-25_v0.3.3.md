## Calendar Sync

You can now subscribe to your confirmed duty bookings in your favourite calendar app. Head to your account settings and enable Calendar Sync to get a personal feed URL. One-click buttons let you add it directly to Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook — or copy the URL and add it manually to any app that supports calendar subscriptions.

Your calendar will automatically stay up to date as bookings are added or changed, and each entry includes the event name, time, location, and a reminder one hour before.

## What's New Page

There's now a dedicated "What's New" page where you can browse the full version history of WirkSam. Each release is listed with its date and a summary of what changed — including screenshots where helpful.

When new versions are released after your last visit, you'll see a notification prompting you to check out what's changed. You can also reach the changelog anytime from the sidebar.

![What's New page showing version history with navigation](./images/changelog.png)

## Localized Screenshots

Screenshots in the changelog now match your language setting. If you're using the app in German, you'll see German screenshots; in English, English screenshots.
