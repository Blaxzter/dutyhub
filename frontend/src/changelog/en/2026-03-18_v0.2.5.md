## Smarter Booking Detection

When you open an event, the app now reliably highlights which duty slots you've already booked. Previously, your existing bookings weren't always detected right away, which could make it confusing to tell whether you'd already signed up for a shift. This is now resolved — your bookings are recognized instantly when the event loads.
