## Print Your Schedule

Need a paper copy of your upcoming duties? There's now a dedicated print view that formats your schedule in a clean, printer-friendly layout. Open it from the schedule page, hit print, and you're done — great for pinning to a notice board or keeping in your planner.

## Multilingual Notifications

Until now, notifications and system messages were always sent in English regardless of your language setting. Starting with this release, all notifications respect your chosen language. If you've set the app to German, your booking confirmations, reminders, and other messages will arrive in German too.
